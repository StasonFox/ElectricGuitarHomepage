Electric Guitar Accessories

Some of the most common guitar accessories are strings, picks, cables, tuners, stands, and cases. Each of these accessory types have their own main brands and their own characteristics. Both strings and picks come in different gauges that create a different tone and feel. Which gauge is best is up to the individual. Cables come in different lengths and are an integral part of playing electric guitar. Tuners are mandatory for keeping guitars in tune and sounding good. Stands and cases are optional, but they are definitely nice to have!

Guitar picks have been made out of feather quills, tortoiseshell, wood, stone, felt, rubber, glass, metal, and different types of plastic.
