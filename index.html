Electric Guitar

One of my favorite hobbies is playing electric guitar. I've been playing it on and off for 18 years (no expert by any means) and it's just something I really enjoy. I'm going to provide an overview of the four main parts of electric guitar: the guitars themselves, amps, pedals, and accessories.

There are many types of electric guitars with many brands. Some of the most famous brands are Gibson, Fender, and Ibanez. The most typical guitar is a six-string, but there are also 7-strings and even higher. Two of the main types of pickups are single-coil and humbuckers. Single-coil have a brighter sound and humbuckers specialize in higher distortion tones (that's why they call them humbuckers: they tame the hum created by high distortion).

The first electric guitar was invented in 1931, but the idea originated in 1890.
