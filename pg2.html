Amps

As with guitars, there are many different types of amps and many brands. Some of the most famous brands are Marshall, Fender, and Peavey. Brands such as Fender specialize in low gain, "clean" tones while Marshalls and Peaveys specialize in higher gain, distorted tones. Most amps have dials that control the amount of gain as well as the bass, mid, and high frequencies that shape the sound. Certain amps, such as Marshalls, really shine when they have a boost pedal in front of them. We will get more into pedals on the next page.

The first electric guitar amplifier was invented in 1932.
