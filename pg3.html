Electric Guitar Pedals

There are so many different variations of guitar pedals that I can only scrape the surface when providing an overview. Some of the main brands include Boss, Electro Harmonix, and Eventide. One of my favorite techniques is to put an overdrive pedal in front of the amp with the gain on the pedal at 0 and the volume maxed. This is one of the tried and true techniques that guitar players have used for decades and it really opens the amp up. Some of the many types of guitar pedal effects are as follows:

  • Distortion
  • Overdrive
  • Fuzz
  • Wah
  • Delay
  • Chorus
  • Reverb
  • EQ
  • Noise Gate
  • Flanger
  • Phaser
  • Tremolo

There are approximately 23 types of guitar pedals.
